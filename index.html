About me

Student Blogs is a free blogs site to help students irrespective of their class to either learn a new skill, or manage your time, and also study tips. I will be posting the blogs twice a month related to the subjects mentioned above and also many more interesting topics.

A little bit about me. I am a high school sophomore currently living in India. Like you guys, I am also a student who have many creative ideas and many ways and tips on studying which I wanted to share with others. I am a beginner in blog writing but experienced in giving advices to others related to studies. I love coding, travelling, acting and also writing. If you want to know how to study effectively, manage your time and many more such things, then please go through my blogs. And yes, I will be posting the blogs twice a month.

Also follow me on instagram at @atharwa_pancholi or else you can also reach out to me through my email address pancholiatharwa@gmail.com for any help related to study advices, time management or anything. You can also share me your ideas of topics which you want me to write about.
