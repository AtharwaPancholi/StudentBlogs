How to Study Effectively?

By Atharwa Pancholi

Almost every student irrespective of their class has this question in mind that how can they study effectively to score better. Believe me, its easy! Its easy if you follow these tips. By using all these techniques, I am able to score better in my school examinations and I hope you will too :)



Tip#1: Pay attention to your Teacher during class




Many times, student don't focus on their classes but it is important to make sure that you pay attention to your teacher. Don't be distracted during your classes and make sure you have understood everything what teacher has taught in the class. So, if you open your book, you don't feel that this is something new.



Tip#2: Take notes




Its very obvious but many students tend to avoid this step. There are many reasons like many of us tend to be lazy during the classes or else we feel that taking notes is not important. But it really is! Always take notes during your classes for future revision and keep them safely in a safe place so you don't have to find them during your exam week. You have to make the notes in such a way that you don't need to open your book before the exam. I will also be writing a blog on How to take notes, so till then stay tuned!



Tip#3: Go through each topic twice or thrice(if possible)




Go through all the chapters of your textbook at least twice and understand it well. If you don't, try taking help from your teacher, or your friends or from youtube. There are many useful youtube videos for almost each chapter. After you have finished reading the chapters, make notes while you study. So you will have two notes in hand, one that you made during class and one that you made by your own. The good thing is that if you have missed any lecture so you can make your notes by your own.



Tip#4: Solve the question/answers




After a clear review of a chapter and making notes, you will be able to solve questions on your own. Try practicing question/answers from different sources like from refreshers, from different websites and many more. Practice all type of questions: Objective, fill ups, true false, very short, long, etc. Also solve some sample papers and assest yourself. In this, you will be able to find your strengths and weaknesses and can also work on your weaknesses by re-reviewing the topic.



Tip#5: Revision




Never forget to revise a topic because as per a study conducted, we tend to forget things which we learn within 3 days. Let us take a practical example, You have studied a chapter thouroughly and have also reviewed the notes. The day you studied the chapter, your knowledge is 100%. But in the second day, it drops down to 70% and 30% on the third day. After three days, it is zero. So what you need to do is that revise the topic you study every fourth day to make your knowledge about the topic from 0 to 100 again.



Tip#6: Teach others




This is the most useful tip. After you are thorough with a particular topic, teach it to your friend or you can teach it to yourself in the mirror. Believe me, if you follow this tip then you are most-likely to ace in a particular topic.



Please do share this blog with your friends wo are struggling with their classes to help them score well. #keepMotivated :)
